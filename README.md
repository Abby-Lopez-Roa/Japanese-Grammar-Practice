# Japanese-Grammar-Practice 🎌

An active-recall study game for **Genki** lessons 19–21 — honorifics, directions, the passive, favors, and more. Built as a single self-contained HTML file: no build step, no dependencies, no data leaves your browser.

> Quizlet × Mad Libs × flashcards, tuned to the actual exam blueprint.

**▶️ Live:** https://github.com/Abby-Lopez-Roa/Japanese-Grammar-Practice

---

## What it drills

Aligned to the **Assessment 3 review sheet** — 54 grammar questions, 25 verb-transformation pairs, and 61 vocabulary cards across 11 topics. Quiz and Exam modes auto-generate extra multiple-choice items from the vocab and transformations, so rounds stay fresh.

## Modes

- **⭐ Mixed Exam** — interleaves every selected topic, closest to the real test
- **⚡ Quick Quiz** — multiple-choice rapid fire
- **📝 Mad Libs** — tap a word from the bank into the blank
- **🃏 Flashcards** — flip and self-grade, for production practice

## Features

- **Furigana toggle** — leave readings on while learning, flip them off to test yourself exam-style
- Score with streak bonuses, 🔥 streak counter, live accuracy %, and a progress bar — **all toggleable**
- A short *why* explanation after every answer
- **"Drill only my misses"** on the results screen to loop your weak spots
- Sound effects + confetti on streak milestones (toggleable)
- Mobile-first; on desktop, keys **1–4** pick answers and **Enter** advances
- Nothing is saved — close and reopen for a clean slate

## How to use

1. Open the live link (or the HTML file) on your phone or laptop.
2. Pick your topics → pick a mode → **はじめる · Start**.
3. Tap **⚙️** anytime to toggle furigana, score, streaks, sound, etc.
